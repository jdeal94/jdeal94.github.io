About Me

From the time I was old enough to walk I began showing a considerable interest in the game of basketball. I had a little plastic goal that couldn’t have stood taller than about 3 feet, and I’d spend hours shooting my little Styrofoam ball into the hoop. I pretty much lived and breathed basketball from that age up until the time I left for college. When I was eight years old, I even told my mom that I was going to make it into the NBA and that I was going to do whatever it took to make my dream a reality. I had hundreds of replica jerseys of all my favorite players and watched them religiously with my dad up until the wee hours of the night. As I got older and realized that no matter how hard I worked, I wouldn’t be able to overcome my lack of size and athleticism, I began directing my energy to other areas such as hiking, fishing, and various outdoor hobbies. I also switched to a more academic approach of finding success as I realized I was pretty capable of doing well in school. Then, I enrolled at UGA and in the chaos of college life I have since all but forgotten my first love, and my first passion. So, this webpage is dedicated to that first passion, the game I fell in love with that as of late, has fallen to the backburner.
